Key concepts in AI, machine learning, and mathematics.

AI Dictionary

  • Algorithm: Step-by-step procedure to solve problems or make decisions.
  • Neural Network: Model inspired by the human brain, used for pattern recognition.
  • Transformer: Deep learning model architecture for sequence data.
  • Reinforcement Learning: Training by rewards from environment interaction.
  • Prompt Engineering: Crafting text to steer AI behavior.
